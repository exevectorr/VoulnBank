Our Story

VulnBank was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: make banking accessible, secure, and transparent for everyone. Starting with just a handful of customers, we've grown into one of the most trusted digital banking platforms, processing over $50 billion in transactions annually.

Our team of 500+ engineers, security experts, and financial analysts work around the clock to ensure your money is safe and your experience is seamless.
